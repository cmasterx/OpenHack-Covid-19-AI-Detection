breather


Breather is a webapp that predicts whether an xray image of the lungs has indication of COVID-19. Using Tensorflow, we trained our AI to recognize healthy and normal lungs with patients with COVID-19.

Warning, this is not medical advice

To use:

  1. Click on Upload X-ray upload a picture of an x-ray of a pair of lungs
  2. Wait for results
  3. Your results should show on the probabilties the lung x-ray is healthy or has COVID-19
